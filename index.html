We are Spacepost,
Mixed Reality Studio,
Based in Los Angeles.

Established in 2002. We produce mixed reality software from our studio in Venice Beach. Our projects include augmented reality curation for museums and gallery openings, web deployable virtual reality tours and sales interfaces, and event production. Working with a broad range of clients ranging from real estate to art museums has given us quite an eclectic portfolio, and we're excited to explore any projects inside the mixed reality medium.
